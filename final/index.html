ABOUT ME

My name is Emily and I am a third year media studies major. Alongside school, photography has been one of my favorite hobbies and creative outlets since I was in junior high. Some of my favorite things to shoot are food, places, and my friends. I put together a photo journal of some of my favorite shots, both digital and film.
